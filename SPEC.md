## 2. Core Components

### Engine (`main.py`)
- **Task Entity:** UUID, Title, Description, is_completed, tags (list), priority (Enum), created_at.
- **Priority Enum:** LOW (1), MEDIUM (2), HIGH (3).
- **TodoManager:** Handles business logic and mutation.
- **Composite Sort:**
    1. Priority (High to Low)
    2. Date (Newest first)
- **StorageManager:** JSON persistence with full hydration of UUID, Datetime, and Enum.
